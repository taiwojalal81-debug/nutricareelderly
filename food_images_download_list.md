# NutriCare Food Images Checklist

Here is the complete list of all 150 food items defined in the app's database seeder. 

You can click the **Unsplash Search Link** to find high-quality, royalty-free images for each dish. Since traditional Nigerian foods might be hard to find on Unsplash, you can also search on Google Images or Pinterest.

> [!TIP]
> **How to name your files:**
> Once you download an image, name the file exactly as the food name, but lowercase with underscores instead of spaces (e.g., for "Amala and Ewedu", name it `amala_and_ewedu.jpg`). 
> Put all the images in a folder and provide them to me, and I will integrate them directly into the app for you!
